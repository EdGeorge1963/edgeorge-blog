Links

Links

These are links, in no particular order, to sites and resources I have found especially useful, both in working my way through Marx, and in trying to understand the world in general.

  • Michael Roberts’ blog (formally The Next Recession). https://thenextrecession.wordpress.com/

  • Alan Freeman on Academia.edu. https://geopoliticaleconomy.academia.edu/AlanFreeman

  • Anwar Shaikh’s Real Economics site. Contains links to a wealth of video class material, and to all of Anwar’s published papers. https://www.realecon.org/

  • Hans Ehrbar’s Annotations to Marx’s Capital. https://content.csbs.utah.edu/~ehrbar/akmc.htm

  • Tony Norfield’s Economics of Imperialism site. https://economicsofimperialism.blogspot.com/

  • Ed George’s Reading Marx Blog (my old reading notes blog). https://readingmarx.wordpress.com/

  • Ed George’s Other Blog (my old non-reading notes blog). https://edgeorgesotherblog.wordpress.com/

  • Reading Marx’s Capital with David Harvey. https://davidharvey.org/

  • Jim Kincaid’s Readings of Capital. https://readingsofcapital.com/

  • Sam Williams’ A Critique of Crisis Theory blog. https://critiqueofcrisistheory.com/
