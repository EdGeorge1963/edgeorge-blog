Of the far-left organisations in South Wales in the 1970s and 80s and beyond, the groups associated with the United Secretariat of the Fourth International (successively the International Marxist Group, Socialist League, International Group and International Socialist Group) were unique in their non-sectarian stance towards the Welsh nationalist movement and their attempts to understand this movement within a broader revolutionary Marxist understanding of socialist politics and strategy. Here are archived a set of the most important texts produced by these organisations over this period. Click on the links below to go to the text in question.
