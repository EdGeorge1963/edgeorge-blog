In November 2004 the British SWP held a dayschool under the title "Capitalism". It was organised in 3 sessions:

* The Transition from Feudalism to Capitalism

* Capitalism Today

* Imperialism

In each session the main speakers were Robert Brenner and someone from the SWP: in the transition session the SWP speaker was Chris Harman, in the capitalism today session Rob Hoveman, and in the imperialism session Alex Callinicos.
